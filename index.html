about

Jennifer Hunt is an independent curator with an honours degree in curating practices from Monash University. Her lens-based art practice explores organic manipulation of the image throughout stages of the silver gelatin process by lens obstruction and exposure materials. She currently works casually with Monash University Museum of Art, volunteers with SYN.fm as a guest host and researcher on Art/Smitten and facilitating after-school art programs.
